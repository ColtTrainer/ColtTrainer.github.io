Final Boss Fights

What makes them good?

Final boss fights in video games are the the culmination of the entire game, and the player needs to feel that they have reached the end of the game, and are expecting to go out with a bang. The boss needs to test everything they have learned while having a powerful atmosphere while wrapping up the story.

How to compare them

Since games have very different mechanics and styles, narrowing down the best boss fights is down to each person's opinions. To compare bosses, it's combining how well they execute things such as mechanics, atmosphere, narrative, etc. in their own genre.

Criteria

These are some criteria I use for judging fights

  • Must be mechanically difficult, fair, and fun to experience
  • Must have a unique design of the boss and the area
  • It makes sense or is satisfying to the story that this is the final boss
  • Music should be good and fitting of the type of fight and story
